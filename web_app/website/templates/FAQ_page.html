FAQ Frequently Asked Questions


General questions

Do I need to create an account in order to run a protein crystal structure through the neural net?

We provide all users the predictions made by the neural net via the email address linked to your account.

Can I submit a protein we crystallized in house?

To submit your own protein crystal structure you must ensure the file format matches the PDB format.
Neural Network Specifics

Who may register for access?

Anyone affiliated with an academic institution may register (email address ending in .edu). If you are not associated with an academic institution please contact us via email: aips@aiproteinsolutions.net

I want run a protein through the neural net - what are the steps?

  • Register for an account.
  • Activate your account via the confirmation email.
  • Login and go to the NN tab and upload your protein structure file or provide the PDB code for the protein.
  • The predictions will be emailed in approximately 30-60 minutes.
