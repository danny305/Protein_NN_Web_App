Neural Network Specifics

Who may register for access?

Anyone may register. Billing is dependent on whether you are affiliated with an academic institution or a company.

I want run a protein through the neural net - what are the steps?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account
  • Activate your account
  • Login and go to the NN section and upload your protein structure file or provide the PDB code for the protein.
  • The results of the Neural Network will be emailed to you.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Paypal. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.

When do I have to pay?

Our standard payment plan provides for monthly payments. The minimum amount of your balance should be at least 70 USD.
